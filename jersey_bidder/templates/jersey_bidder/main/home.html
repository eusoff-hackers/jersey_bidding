Rules governing bidding priorities


Priorities to bid for Jersey Numbers are determined by the following factors:


1. All Eusoffians will be grouped according to number of years he or she has participated in IHG

This is the first determining factor - seniors will be given higher priorities over juniors in choosing numbers and seniors will be given a number before the juniors.


2. Within the same batch, priority points will then be awarded

  • Number of sports an Eusoffian is participating in after the first cut - 1 point per sport
  • Leadership position (if any) held in Eusoff sports team - 2 points
  • If the Eusoffian is representing teamNUS - 2 points


    • 3. If there are still ties, the Eusoffians with the same priority points will be randomly sorted

    Under the preference page, you can see which numbers are popular. You can also indicate your preference to de-conflict with other Eusoffians.


    Take note, indication of prefernce is not an official bidding request.

    Once you have decicded, go to the bid page to submit your top 5 choices.
